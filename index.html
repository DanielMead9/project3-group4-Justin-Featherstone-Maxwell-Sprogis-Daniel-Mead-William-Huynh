AV Repair (All Vehicle Repair) is a Massachusetts licensed and bonded repair shop.

We service cars and trucks of all ages, gasoline, diesel and electric, both domestic and foreign, including GMC, Chevrolet, Buick, Cadillac, Pontiac, Oldsmobile, Ford, Chrysler, Dodge, Plymouth, BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Volkswagen, Porsche, Fiat, Volvo, Saab, Toyota, Lexus, Nissan, Infiniti, Honda, Acura, Subaru, Mazda, Hyundai, Kia, Jaguar, Land Rover, Range Rover, and more.

We're here during these trying times, to perform the repairs or service your car needs and we'll pick up your car from your home or workplace and deliver it back to you.

We Have ASE Certified Technicians

What is ASE and why is it important? ASE, is short for the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence.. ASE is an independent non-profit organization that works to improve the quality of vehicle repair and service by testing and certifying automotive professionals to protect the automotive service consumer.

Our technicians hold 7 ASE certifications:

  • Engine Repair
  • Manual Drive Train & Axles
  • Suspension and Steering
  • Brakes
  • Electrical/Electronic Systems
  • Heating and Air Conditioning
  • Engine Performance
